MOZKITO Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-09

English

MOZKITO is an entertainment app for Android phones and Wear OS watches. It displays a mosquito-themed screen and can play a locally generated high-frequency tone when the user activates it.

Data collection

MOZKITO does not collect personal data.

The app does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data, location, contacts, photos, files, microphone audio, camera data, health data, app analytics, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers for advertising.

Data sharing

MOZKITO does not share user data with third parties.

Ads, analytics, accounts, and network access

  • No ads.
  • No analytics.
  • No crash-reporting SDK.
  • No account system.
  • No login.
  • No network calls.
  • No Android INTERNET permission.

Audio

When activated, MOZKITO generates a tone locally on the device using native Android audio APIs. The app does not record audio and does not use the microphone.

Entertainment disclaimer

MOZKITO is an entertainment app. It does not guarantee mosquito repellent effects and is not a substitute for repellents, mosquito nets, window screens, or health guidance.
